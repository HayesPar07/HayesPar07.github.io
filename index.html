Shrimp Ceviche

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds peeled raw shrimp, diced Juice of five limes 2 tablespoons jalapeños, seeded and chopped 1/4 cup red onion, minced 1 Cucumber peeled, seeded and diced 2 Avocados, diced 1 bunch cilantro, chopped Dash salt.

Procedure

Rinse shrimp under cold water and pat dry. Place shrimp in a large zip-lock bag with lime juice, jalapeño, red onion, ans salt. Toss and refrigerate one hour or until shrimpare opaque. When ready to serve toss in cucumber and avocado. Serve with tortilla chips.

Creamy Crab and Artichoke Dip

Ingredients

1 (8-ounce) cream cheese, room temperature 1 cup mayonnaise 1 package imitation crabmeat 1/3 cup chopped onion 1/3 can (13 3/4-ounce) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped 3/4 cup (3-ounces) shredded Parmesan cheese.

Procedure

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In medium mixing bowl, blend together the cream cheese and mayonnaise until smooth. Stir in remaining ingredients and place mixture in glass pie plate. Bake uncovered for 15 to 18 minutes, until heated throughout and lightly brown. Serve with crackers or french bread.
